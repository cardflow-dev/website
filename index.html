What Your Team Will Build

Issue Payment Cards

Create personalized payment cards using JavaCard technology. Understand card chip architecture and payment application deployment.

Process Real Transactions

Use professional NFC readers to process contactless payments. Print custom receipts with your company logo.

Operate Both Sides

Run issuer (bank) and acquirer (processor) systems. Experience the complete authorization flow from both perspectives.

Master ISO 8583

Deep dive into the messaging standard that powers global payment networks. Build, route, and process real payment messages.

Why This Workshop Matters

Most engineers see only fragments of the payment ecosystem. They might understand acquiring at a high level, or work with issuing APIs, but rarely experience both sides of the network. This workshop changes that. Your team will understand how their code fits into the bigger picture, make better architectural decisions, and build more robust payment integrations.
